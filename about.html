About Me

Hello ! I am Aniket Dash. I recently graduated with a degree in data science and completed my data science internship with CropIn.

I have always been in love with stories and how they map thoughts into existence.

My ability to see connections between ideas allows me to view the world through a unifying lens.

Brief History

Till 2018

Born in Sunabeda, Koraput, I wasn’t the top of my class, but I was endlessly curious and that helped me stay above water. I was quite good at all kinds of sports and movement.

2019-2021

After school, I got into OUTR( formerly CET) to study Applied Physics. But I mostly found myself playing guitar in the college band that’s where I spent the majority of my time (and heart).

2021-2025

I eventually dropped out and joined IIT, Madras to study data science. I started to learn to code from scratch, revisited complex mathematics and naturally got gravitated towards deep learning. The realization that a model's ability to learn and adapt is inspired from the human brain acted as a catalyst for me to get deeper into it.

My internship at CropIn was a great experience that allowed me to dive deeper into earth observation. With guidance from Ravikanta Bhargava and Deshwani E, I worked on cloud removal networks and contrastive learning methods like SimCLR for crop monitoring.
